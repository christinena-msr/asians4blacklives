Northwest Community Bail Fund

The Northwest Community Bail Fund works in Seattle to help pay bail for prisoners who are not able to make bail themselves. In light of the pandemic, making bail is especially important as social distancing is not possible in jails and therefore puts people at greater risk of infection. Their greater goal is continued de-carceration in Seattle and King County.

LEGAL FUNDS

NAACP Legal Defense Fund

The NAACP Legal Defense Fund fights to protect legal rights and voters and seeks to create structural change to achieve racial justice and equality. Their work extends from criminal justice and police reform to education to protection of Black communities against COVID-19.

ACLU - COVID

The American Civil Liberties Union works to preserve individual rights and liberties guaranteed by the Constitution. Their current projects are defunding the police, ensuring reparations for slavery, and expanding voting access during the COVID-19 pandemic. This particular link is focused on releasing vulnerable populations in prisons, jails, and detention centers to practice social distancing, ensuring access to vote-by-mail, and keeping reproductive health care centers open.

ORGANIZATIONS

The Marshall Project

Journalism is an essential part of exposing stories, good or bad, that might otherwise be overlooked. The Marshall Project examines the American criminal justice system in particular, remaining open-minded and focused on the facts of the situation. Notable topics they dive in to are the death penalty, immigration, and mental health.

Black Lives Matter - Seattle Chapter

This is the official site for the Black Lives Matter chapter in the Seattle-King County area. BLM Seattle King Country focuses on demolishing anti-black systems and policies. They have created the Seattle Freedom Fund as a means of bailout support, which is currently fully funded. You can use this link to support the chapter’s general fund.
