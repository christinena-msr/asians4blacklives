Register to Vote

Voting is one of the most important things we can do as Americans. We the people get to decide who our representatives are, from local officials to the president of the country. All of these elections are important because these representatives are able to influence each part of our lives, even if we do not see it. You may think your vote will not matter because yours is one amongst millions, but voting is what keeps our democracy alive and causes change.
